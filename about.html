Jesse's Awesome Blog is a website dedicated to housing my personal anime/manga rankings and music reviews. The strict system used to rate these listings is called the Jesse Scale™. Some of the criteria for the Jesse Scale™ includes artistic value, overarching message, and vibes. If you have any questions or disagree with a ranking/review try reconsidering.
